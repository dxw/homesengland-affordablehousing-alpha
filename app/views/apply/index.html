Apply for affordable housing funding

Existing and prospective Strategic Partners with Homes England should use this service to:

  • check if you are eligible for funding
  • apply for funding

Applying for funding means you intend to enter into a long term arrangement with Homes England to deliver homes over a period of time.

This service is for funding from 2021 to 2026 and replaces all previous services.

Check our Capital Funding Guide for help on information you’ll need to provide in your application.

Before you start

To apply for funding you’ll need to:

  • be an investment partner with Homes England or applying jointly with an investment partner
  • agree to deliver a minimum number of homes
  • certify that your application has been approved by your organisation’s board of directors

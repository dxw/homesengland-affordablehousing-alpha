Submit a proposal for affordable housing funding

Use this service to apply for funding of your building scheme in the financial years 2021 to 2026, and sometimes to 2028.

All homes funded by this scheme need to be delivered by the end of March 2028, and sometimes March 2029.

Check our Capital Funding Guide for help on information you’ll need to provide in your application.

Before you start

To apply for funding you’ll need to:

  • be an investment partner with Homes England or applying jointly with an investment partner
  • agree to deliver a minimum number of homes
  • certify that your application has been approved by your organisation’s board of directors
